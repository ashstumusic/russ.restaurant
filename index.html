Our Story Real food,
cooked right.

✦ ✦ ✦

We bread our fish and chicken by hand every single day, fry it up in seasoned cast iron, and put it on your plate with sides made from scratch.

No fancy plating. No online reservations. Just a family-run kitchen on Cherokee Street that's been feeding folks who know the difference between good food and everything else.

Call ahead and we'll have it hot when you walk in.
